Policy

We work across a multitude of nutrition policy issues from crop fortification to childhood obesity. We uncover a broad and often complex evidence base, and identify real-world solutions. We turn findings into practical policy recommendations that push the boundaries towards a healthy and fair food distribution.

Our services include literature and systematic reviews, research projects, stakeholder engagement, intervention evaluation and data modelling.

Advocacy

We harness the power of creativity and ambitious thinking in our advocacy work, seeking out innovative and rapid ways to address issues including chronic diseases, food quality, and consumer behaviour.

We produce creative content that pushes our clients messages to the top of agendas. We create cross-sector partnerships, bringing organisations together to work on common goals. Our advocacy accelerates the pace of change for a healthier and fairer future.

Our services include developing strategies and campaign materials, report writing, partnership strategies and creative content development.

Behaviour Change

We apply a powerful mix of scientific rigor and behavioural psychology to change the behaviour of people, communities and the producers of food. We develop innovative models that are nimble and scalable.

Our services include visioning and goal setting, stakeholder engagement, intervention planning, pilot and feasibility studies. We also undertake data collection and evaluation.

Health Improvement

We recognise that in today’s world making changes to food consumption can feel really challenging for people and institutions. We work with our clients to make simple do-able changes that address issues of chronic diseases and promote health.

Our services include schools programmes, community workshops, corporate wellness, 1-1 nutrition coaching.
