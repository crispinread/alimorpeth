Our Vision is Simple:

a world where a fair and healthy distribution of food is shared by everybody. The challenges we face are systemic, but we have the tools and knowledge we need at our disposal.

As a Registered Nutritionist (RNutr) with a creative flair, Ali seeks out new pathways for innovation to get nutrition solutions to scale, fast. Ali has 15 years experience of consulting on projects of various sizes - from analysing primary school menus in West Yorkshire to the UN’s Sustainable Development Goals.
